About Us

So Share is a collaborative co-working space for those in the creative, content & digital space. For a monthly membership fee we provide a working environment with high speed internet, unlimited tea & coffee, additional access to conference rooms bookable on an ad hoc basis and plenty more additional features.


Flexibility, connections, clarity and environment are the key values of the coshare, inviting individuals and groups to “get things done better, faster, and seize new opportunities” within our buzzing environment.


This is a place where ambitious minds and problem solvers come together to make things happen. It's a coworking space that's built for you. Innovative, creative, bold. Because you deserve a workspace that's worthy of your talents.
